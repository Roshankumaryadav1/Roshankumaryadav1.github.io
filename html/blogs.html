Affect of technology in our Daily lives

Without technology we cannot survive for the single second. For example if we want to travel from one place to another we need the help of vehicle, If we want entertainment we need the help of mobile, pc, laptop etc, If we want to explore the world we need the help of television etc and all of this are the technology.


Not only this if we want to buy tickets,book hotels,do shopping we can do it from internet online. Science and Technoloy has made our life Easier, Faster, and more fun. Technology has helped us to connect to the people with ease and has made many tasks so easier. We can Communicate from one end of the world to another end of the world with just click in one button.It has made our life more convinent. It is ver happy to see the growing of technology in our life. If technology will stop changing than our life will stop progressing.
